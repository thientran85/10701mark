MLS Fees – Reminder!

MLS fees were due on Wednesday, January 8th, 2014.  Those who did not pay on the scheduled date incurred a $40 late fee.

All fees due – MLS fees and late fee - need to be received by 5pm Tuesday, January 21st, to avoid account deactivation. 

In order to have your MLS account reactivated, you will be required to pay the MLS dues plus a $100.00 reinstatement fee.  To avoid disruption in service and to avoid being charged the reinstatement fee, please visit the payment site at  https://ebrd.rapams.com to confirm you have paid all fees due.

If you need assistance making your payment, please contact EBRD’s Customer Support at (925) 363-2333 between the hours of 8:30am and 5:00pm, Monday thru Friday.

Mobile MLS Access For iPhone, iPad, and Android

Point your Apple or Android device to your app store and download the new “Goomzee” mobile MLS app.  East Bay Regional Data is pleased to announce that you may access agent-only listing data, including showing instructions, on your smart device now using native mobile apps.  Features include nearby search, criteria search, address or MLS# quick search.  You may also search the MLS roster, and view local neighborhood amenities.  Additional Paragon integration is in the works so be the first to check it out.

After downloading “Goomzee” app from your app store, select "My Account" icon (upper right hand corner), select "Agent Login", select "E. Bay Area" and then select "Login now" to enter your MLS credentials.

  

Notification:  New Paragon Feature

On Monday, December 16th, LPS will activate an Expiration Alert feature within Paragon 5.

The Expiration Alert system monitors inventory requiring immediate attention, so that you do not have to.  Paragon will email notifications to the listing agent of Active properties, which are due to expire in less than seven days time.  This reminder will allow ample time to contact the client regarding necessary paperwork to extend or relist, if required.

Please always ensure the email address on your Paragon account is current, as this is the primary means of EBRD’s communication with you.
